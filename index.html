    Women Helpline

    Objectives

    • To provide Digital Complaint Registration System for women in distress through trained counsellors
    • To facilitate psychological counselling or referral to the appropriate agencies such as Police, Hospitals, District Legal Service Authority (DLSA), Protection Officer (PO), One Stop Crisis Centre (OSC).
    • To provide information about the appropriate support services, government agencies, etc.
    • For any emergency assistance please call our women helpline number 112.

    Women Helpline

    Services

    Receives thousands of complaints every year related to Domestic Violence and other issues. Such incidents can make an adverse impact on mental health and sometimes they need psychological support to get out of this trauma.

    Any woman or girl (above 18 yrs) in distress seeking help can call this helpline. The helpline is staffed by Psychological Counsellors trained in topmost Mental Health and Counselling institutions.

    About SURAKSHA

    The SURAKSHA, is the statutory body of the Government of India, generally concerned with advising the government on all policy matters affecting women. The objective of the SURAKSHA is to represent the rights of women in India and to provide a voice for their issues and concerns. The subjects of the campaigns run by SURAKSHA have included dowry, politics, religion, equal representation for women in jobs, and the exploitation of women for labour.
